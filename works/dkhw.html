title="Dragking Highway (ENGSUB)">

Dragking Highway is a DIY digital road-movie, exploring the different textures and temporalities of transfem hitch-hiking.

Mundane conversations of varying degrees of truth are layered over landscapes of never ending highways, recreated gas-stations and compressed horizon lines.

This film is a product of iteration, a patchwork of footage and sounds captured over the span of three years, processed and digested through screens and different digital tools. The mixed media approach to this film is heavily inspired by guerrilla filmmaking, this time using archival and video-game development tools as a way to bypass the financial, technical and legal constraints of cinema. The result is a non-linear narrative contrasting truth and lies, recollections and reconstructions, domesticity and vulnerability.
